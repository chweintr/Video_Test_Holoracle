PastPresence

An interactive heritage platform that lets visitors talk with the past. Rather than passive exhibits or static text, people engage in real-time dialogue with historical figures, powered by contextual AI, period-accurate voice synthesis, and expressive avatar technology.

Echoes of Indiana

The flagship installation surfaces voices from the state's layered history. A 1917 furniture factory worker. A Hoosier poet. A limestone quarry channeler. An RCA quality inspector. Even the legendary Brown County Bigfoot.

Each persona draws from researched historical context, regional dialect, and period-specific knowledge. The result is not a chatbot. It is an encounter. A chance to ask questions that textbooks cannot answer.

History is not just what happened. It is who was there.
